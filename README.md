# 📊 Regional Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a professional Power BI dashboard built to analyze **regional sales performance, profitability, and business trends**.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

* Which regions generate the most revenue
* Which sales channels are most effective
* Who the top-performing salespeople are
* How sales trends change over time

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Companies often lack visibility into:

* Regional sales performance
* Profitability drivers
* Sales team effectiveness

This dashboard solves these challenges by providing **interactive and actionable insights**.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

* Order details (Order ID, Date)
* Region & State
* Customer and Salesperson
* Product Category & Sales Channel
* Revenue, Cost, Profit, Discount, Quantity

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

* 💰 Total Revenue: 290M+
* 📊 Total Profit: 87M+
* 📦 Total Orders: 2000+
* 📉 Profit Margin: 30%

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* East and South regions are top performers
* Sales peak in early months (Q1 trend)
* Revenue is evenly distributed across sales channels
* Top salespeople contribute significantly to revenue
* Customer concentration indicates key account dependency
